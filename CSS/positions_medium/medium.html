Chiweenies

Chiweenies are a mix between a Chihuahua and a weiner dog. I happen to have a chiweenie and they are a very special breed. The sweet temperament comes from their Daschund side. The shyness comes from their Chihuahua side. Hopefully this is enough wording to actually show up in my main window. I don't think it will be enugh to scroll.If it isn't, I'll add that Latin.
